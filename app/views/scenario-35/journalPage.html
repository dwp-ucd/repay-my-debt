As you are repaying money to DWP in relation to previous claims, you can use the new Repay and manage benefit money you owe service.

You will receive a text message from DWP Debt Management in the next 7 days with details of how to sign in to yourÂ Repay and manage benefit money you owe account.

On the new service you can view a breakdown of what you owe to DWP and manage your payments.

The money you owe to DWP is in relation to previous claims and appears under 'Debts and loans repayments' on your Universal Credit statement.

For more information and a full breakdown of deductions from your Universal Credit payment, you can check your Universal Credit statement.

It is important to understand that any other money currently being deducted from your Universal Credit payment which is not related to previous claims, cannot be managed on the new service. This may include any advances taken out as part of a new claim.
