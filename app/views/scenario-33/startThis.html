Repay and manage benefit money you owe

Use this service if you need to manage your benefit payments or repay money to the Department of Work and Pensions (DWP).

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

If you're currently claiming benefits, you can use this service to:

  • view details of how much you owe
  • view a breakdown of your monthly deductions
  • make additional payments
  • find help and support for repaying what you owe

If you've recently stopped claiming benefits, you can use this service to:

  • set up regular monthly payments
  • manage an existing Direct Debit or flexible payment plan
  • pay back what you owe in full
  • find help and support for repaying what you owe
There’s also a way to repay benefit money to the Department for Communities if you live in Northern Ireland.

Before you start

You'll need to have received a letter or text message from DWP inviting you to use the service.
