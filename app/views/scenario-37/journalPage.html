You can use a new service to manage the money you owe to DWP

As you are repaying money to DWP, you can use the new Repay and manage benefit money you owe service.

Go to https://www.gov.uk/repay-manage-benefit-owed then sign in to your account using security code 3005487625, along with your date of birth.

On the new service you can view a breakdown of what you owe to DWP and manage your payments.

The money you owe to DWP appears under 'Debts and loans repayments' on your Universal Credit statement.

As you are in a joint claim with your partner, your benefit deductions will be used to repay any money you both owe.

For more information and a full breakdown of deductions from your Universal Credit payment, you can check your Universal Credit statement.

It is important to understand that any other money currently being deducted from your Universal Credit payment cannot be managed on the new service. This may include any advances taken out as part of a new claim.

If you have any questions, you should contact DWP Debt Management contact center directly:
