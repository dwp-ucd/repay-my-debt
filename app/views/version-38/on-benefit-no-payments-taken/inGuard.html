Before you start

You will need:

  • an email address
  • a mobile phone

You will also need information from 2 sources so we can check we are dealing with the right person. These are:

  • a UK passport
  • payslips or P60M
  • information about tax credits, if you claim them
  • details of your credit record

What happens next

Your information will be checked against our records. This also helps protect you from fraud.

We will send security codes to your email and mobile phone to check that they belong to you.

Whenever you sign in we will send a new security code to your mobile phone.

You will also need to create a password.
